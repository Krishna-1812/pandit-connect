How It Works

Your spiritual journey made simple in four easy steps

Select Your Puja

Browse our extensive catalogue and pick the ritual that aligns with your life event or spiritual intention. Unsure which to choose? Our experts are ready to advise you.

Schedule Your Ceremony

Choose a convenient date, time and location. We accommodate early mornings, auspicious muhurats and weekend slots to fit your routine.

Get Matched

Our smart matching pairs you with a vetted pandit who specialises in your puja, speaks your language and understands your cultural nuances.

Experience the Ritual

Sit back as your pandit performs the ceremony with devotion. Join in person or via live stream and receive prasad delivered to your door.
